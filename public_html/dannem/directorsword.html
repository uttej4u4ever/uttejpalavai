THE DIRECTOR'S WORD
 

I feel proud to say that I am product of The Nagarjuna University, did my P.G at campus college, Nagarjuna Nagar, Guntur. After my P.G I left for Hyderabad to join in direction department. To find a room I struggled for years, but invain. Under 'can't but' conditions I joined in a corporate college as teaching assistant, where I was blocked for three years. Atlast I find a place to work with 'CHANDU' for his debut film '10th CLASS' as an assistant director.

In my opinion an unmitigated passion for movies and script making power are enough to direct a film than to work for so many pictures/ so many years in direction department to any one. I went through my skills, standards, abilities and cross examined in all factors, after number of multiplications, I resolved to take a chance for direction, exactly at this point I have tasted the core part of struggles and insults. Some mocked (the people who have struck in film industry in search of offers with abundant exerience for years) at me. It gave me a chance to re-estimate my abilities and sources. I strengthed and made up my mind much better. Later I have approached a producer and discussed.
Working for years/ to somany pictures/ with somany directors is not compulsory to become a director.
 
 
 
 

SRI NARA SIMHA
